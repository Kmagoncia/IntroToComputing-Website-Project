About Me!

Born in Misamis Oriental, raised in Kabacan, North Cotabato. Life, to me, is an art – I paint it with vibrant colors, embracing challenges with a perpetual smile. An avid enthusiast, I find joy in unraveling life's mysteries and welcoming the future. My resilience lies in tackling new challenges, as I believe both success and failures shape our journey. I find solace in reading to ease stress, exploring new worlds through fiction and comics. Music isn't just a hobby; it's a profound teacher, guiding me through self-discovery, fostering connections, and embracing diverse cultures.
